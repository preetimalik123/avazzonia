Dubai City Tour

Day 3

Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner

Experience the magic and mystery of an Arabian desert 4WD tour. The safari will give you the golden opportunity to see the towering sand dunes of the desert as you go on a roller coaster ride onboard your all-terrain vehicle. This tour provides a thrilling and fun-filled experience in the most remote desert area known as Lahbab. Experience the skills of your driver as they drive across the rolling sand dunes for 30-40 minutes.

Stop at the highest dune to watch a desert sunset. Back on board, continue the drive to a Bedouin campsite right in the heart of the desert. When you arrive at camp you will be greeted with Qahwa (Arabic coffee) and dates. Your safari guide will brief you about the various available activities at the camp. You can hop-on the camel for a short ride, smoke a shisha, decorate your hands with Henna tattoos, and also can take pictures in traditional Arabic clothes.

As the night settles in, enjoy the scrumptious BBQ dinner of kebabs, hummus, and salads accompanied by sodas/soft drinks and water. Alcoholic drinks are available with additional charges. Enjoy a colorful belly dancing, Fire show, and Tanura performance where fast-spinning dancers’ whirl across the stage. Don’t forget to snap some photos with the Arabian falcons that are present at the camp (might be additional charges). Finally, after the last show, you will be driven back to your hotel.
